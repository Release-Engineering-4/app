Phishing Link detector

Link phishing, also known as phishing through malicious links, involves cyberattacks where attackers send deceptive emails or messages containing links to fraudulent websites. These sites mimic legitimate platforms to trick users into revealing sensitive information like usernames, passwords, or credit card numbers.
This tool is designed by Team 04 of the course Release Engineering for Machine Learning Applications 2024 to help you detect phishing links. Simply enter the link you want to check in the input field below and click the "Check" button.
